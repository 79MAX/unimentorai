# 2. General Refund Principle

Due to the nature of digital educational content and AI-powered services, most purchases are considered **non-refundable once accessed or consumed**.

However, we provide limited refund eligibility under specific conditions described below.

# 3. Eligible Refund Cases

You may be eligible for a refund if:

* you were charged incorrectly or twice;
* you were unable to access the purchased service due to a verified technical error on our side;
* the purchase was made fraudulently without your authorization;
* the service purchased was not delivered as described;
* the refund request is made within the eligible refund window (see Section 5).

---

# 4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following cases:

* completion of more than a minimal portion of a course;
* consumption of digital content (videos, AI lessons, certificates);
* dissatisfaction with learning outcomes;
* change of mind after purchase;
* failure to complete a course within the allowed time;
* violation of our Terms of Service leading to account suspension;
* promotional or discounted purchases (unless required by law).

# 5. Refund Time Window

Refund requests must generally be submitted within:

* **7 days** for subscriptions;
* **14 days** for one-time course purchases (depending on jurisdiction).

Requests submitted after this period may not be eligible.
