# 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website or use an application.

They allow our platform to recognize your device, remember your preferences, improve performance, and enhance security.

Similar technologies may also include:

* Local Storage
* Session Storage
* Web Beacons
* Tracking Pixels
* SDK Identifiers
* Similar browser technologies

Cookies generally do not directly identify you. However, they may be associated with information linked to your account.
