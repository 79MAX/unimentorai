# Refund Policy – UniMentorAI

**Effective Date:** 2026  
**Platform:** UniMentorAI (AI Learning & Certification System)

---

## 1. Introduction

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which UniMentorAI may issue refunds for purchases made on the platform.

By making a purchase, you agree to this policy.

---

## 2. Refund Eligibility

A refund may be granted only if ALL the following conditions are met:

- The refund request is made within **7 days** of purchase
- The course has NOT been fully completed
- No certificate has been issued or generated
- The digital content has not been fully consumed

---

## 3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following cases:

- Completed courses
- Issued certificates
- Fully accessed or consumed digital content
- Abuse of the refund system
- Fraudulent or suspicious activity
- Violation of Terms of Service

---

## 4. Third-Party Payment Providers

Payments are processed through third-party providers:

- Kkiapay
- Stripe
- PayPal
- Mobile Money providers (MTN, Orange Money, Moov Money)

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used during purchase.

---

## 5. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, users must:

- Contact official support
- Provide the transaction ID
- Provide the email used during purchase

Refund requests are typically processed within **3 to 10 business days**.

---

## 6. Partial Refunds

In certain exceptional cases, UniMentorAI may offer:

- Partial refunds
- Platform credit
- Alternative course access

These decisions are made on a case-by-case basis.

---

## 7. Digital Products Policy

All UniMentorAI content is considered digital products.

Therefore:

- Once a course is fully consumed, it is non-refundable
- Issued certificates are final and non-refundable
- Access to digital content is considered delivery of service

---

## 8. Fraud and Abuse Prevention

UniMentorAI reserves the right to refuse any refund request in cases of:

- Refund system abuse
- Multiple account creation
- Attempted payment manipulation
- Suspicious or fraudulent activity

---

## 9. Service Cancellation

UniMentorAI may cancel or suspend purchases in cases of:

- Major technical issues
- Policy violations
- Fraud detection or system abuse

In such cases, refunds may be issued automatically where applicable.

Continued use of the platform after updates means acceptance of the revised policy.
