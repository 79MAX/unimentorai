# UniMentorAI – Commercial Pitch Template (PowerPoint/Google Slides)

---

## Slide 1 – Cover
- Logo UniMentorAI
- Tagline (e.g. “Empowering Education, Certifying Success”)
- Contact info (email, website)

---

## Slide 2 – Vision & Mission
- Vision statement
- Mission statement
- 1–2 impactful stats (education, Africa, digital)

---

## Slide 3 – The Problem
- Key pain points (skills gap, certification, accessibility, cost)
- Quotes or stats from target market

---

## Slide 4 – Our Solution
- UniMentorAI in 1–2 sentences
- Key differentiators (certification, multilingual, RGPD, premium, Africa focus)
- Visual: platform screenshot or diagram

---

## Slide 5 – Product Overview
- Main features (courses, certification, mentoring, analytics, admin panel)
- Visual: feature icons or screenshots

---

## Slide 6 – Pricing & Offers
- Table: Segments (Schools, Enterprises, NGOs), Tiers (Starter, Pro, Premium)
- Volume discounts, regional pricing
- Visual: pricing comparison card

---

## Slide 7 – Security & Compliance
- RGPD compliance, data security, certifications
- Accessibility (WCAG), internationalization
- Visual: badges/logos

---

## Slide 8 – Success Stories / References
- 2–3 client logos or testimonials (real or placeholders)
- Impact metrics (users, certificates delivered, etc.)

---

## Slide 9 – Roadmap & Innovation
- Upcoming features (AI, gamification, analytics, etc.)
- Timeline visual

---

## Slide 10 – Team & Partners
- Key team members (photos, roles)
- Strategic partners (logos)

---

## Slide 11 – Call to Action
- “Request a demo”, “Contact us”, “Get a quote”
- Contact info, QR code (link to demo or website)

---

## Visual & Design Tips
- Use UniMentorAI brand colors and fonts
- Prefer visuals/icons over text blocks
- Keep slides clean, 1 idea per slide
- Use real screenshots where possible
- End with a strong, clear call-to-action

---

*This template can be imported into PowerPoint or Google Slides. Each section = 1 slide.*
